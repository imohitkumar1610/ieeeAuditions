What is IEEE

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

  • Over 409,000 members in more than 160 countries, more than 60 percent of whom are from outside the United States
  • More than 125,000 Student members
  • 3,182 Student Branch Chapters of IEEE technical Societies

IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. IEEE is the trusted “voice” for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe.

What is IEEE SB-nit Dgp


The IEEE Student Branch, NIT Durgapur is a society of enthusiasts aimed at promoting research-related activities in the campus.

We are a direct handshake to IEEE, an international body that allows countless young researchers the opportunity to present & publish their innovations every year. Comprising of bright researchers, developers, speakers, and other contributors we are a society that welcomes in the era of better research prospects, on the campus.


  • We promote research culture among tomorrows world.
  • We provide a world wide platform.
  • We innovate we invent.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is IEEE ?

    IEEE is Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers which is the world's largest technical group with engineering professionals united from all over the world to continuously learn, interact, collaborate and innovate. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its technology standards and educational activities.

  • Why IEEE ?

    It's important to network and expand your base of knowledge in order to bring new thoughts into play, joining IEEE is one way to broaden your horizons as it has more than 4 lakhs active members and 40 technical societies.

  • Are there any prerequisites for the audition?

    There is no such prerequisite to get into IEEE, you can apply irrespective of your branch and expertise. We evaluate your urge to learn and work as a team. Your interest and enthusiasm is what matters the most!

  • Criteria upon which selection is done ?

    Interest in the club and its activities. Eagerness to learn. Soft skills such as teamwork, leadership, confidence.

  • What are domains in IEEE?

    IEEE has the following domains :

    • Technology
    • Web Development
    • Content Writing
    • Graphic Designing
    • Video Editing
    • Management
